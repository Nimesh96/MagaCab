❓ How to Use MagaCab

Follow these steps to book your ride.

  1. Go to the **User Dashboard**.
  2. Click **"Book a Ride"** to schedule a new trip.
  3. Check **"Booking History"** to view past bookings.
  4. Visit **"Payments"** to complete any pending payments.
  5. If you need any assistance, contact our support.
